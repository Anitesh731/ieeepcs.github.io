The childhood which comprised entirely of cartoons and game shows?\nThese television treats have been an exciting and unifying part of all of our happy days. Not sure about other shows, but almost everyone eagerly waited for the clock to strike 9 to watch Takeshi\u2019s Castle.\nThe amazing rounds, the hilarious Japanese participants, the ever enthusiastic General Lee and Javed Jaffrey\u2019s gut-busting commentary; Takeshi\u2019s Castle had us all laughing out loud. Haven\u2019t we all loved that show and secretly wished for a similar show in India?\nWe can\u2019t create that amazing gauntlet, or those skipping stones, or arrange some high rollers, but we sure can have you experience the Final Showdown. Water Guns, shooting each other, getting some money; who in their right mind won\u2019t want that?\nJoin us with your friends this Riviera in our very own version of Takeshi\u2019s Castle.
